Design is not just what it looks like, it's how it works.

Hello! I'm Sharmila Pyatha, a passionate and imaginative graphic designer with a fresh perspective and a keen eye for detail. I love turning ideas into visuals that not only look great but also communicate effectively and leave a lasting impression. My journey in design has been fueled by curiosity, creativity, and a commitment to understanding peoples needs. I believe that design is more than aesthetics its about solving problems, telling stories, and creating experiences that truly resonate.

From crafting brand identities and logos to designing print layouts, editorial pieces, and digital interfaces, I enjoy exploring different mediums to bring ideas to life. I'm also drawn to thoughtful use of typography, color theory, and layout because the small details often make the biggest impact.
